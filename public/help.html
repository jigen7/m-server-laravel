• How to Search User

  • Go to your profile.
  • Tap at the top right, next to settings.
  • Type in the name or email address of the person you want to search for in the search bar.
  • Tap at the right side of the search bar.
  • Results will be shown below the search bar.

  • How to Follow Friends

    • There are a few ways to follow your friends.
    1. Through our Auto Suggest Follow.
  • After you sign up, you are shown a list of your Facebook friends who use Masarap app. This gives you an option to follow them.
  • Tap Follow All if you want to follow all your suggested friends.
  • Tap if you wish to select friends you want to follow.
    • 2. Search User then Follow.
    If you have specific friends in mind you can search for them (see how to search user).
  • On the search result
  • Tap the at the right side of the item result.
  • Or go to their profile, then tap the Follow button.

  • How to unFollow Friends

    • The simple way to unfollow friends is through your following list.
  • Go to your profile.
  • Tap Following, below your name.
  • Tap at the right side of a name you want to unfollow.
  • Confirm your decision.

  • How to find Restaurants

    • There are a few ways to find restaurants.
    1. The first view of the Masarap application is a list of restaurants nearest you. Use it to choose the best restaurant.
  • Explore each tab (Distance, Rating, Cuisine, Budget).
  • Use Distance tab if you're looking for a good restaurant that is closest to where you are.
  • Use Rating tab if you want to select the restaurant with the highest rating.
  • If you have a specific cuisine type in mind, use Cuisine tab.
  • OR if you are on a budget, use Budget tab.
    • 2. Use the Map.
  • Tap anywhere on the map to make it full view.
  • The indicates that there’s a restaurant at that location.
  • Move the map to show more restaurants near you.
  • Tap to show restaurant information.
  • Tap the restaurant information overlay to show restaurant page for more info.
    • 3. If you have a specific restaurant in mind, use Search Tab to find Restaurant.
  • Go to Search Tab.
  • Type in the name of the restaurant, or if you don’t know the exact name of the restaurant just type any keyword about that restaurant (eg: cake, ice cream, etc).
  • Tap to show result.

  • How to get directions going to the restaurant

  • Go to restaurant page.
  • In the restaurant information, tap the directions.
  • It will redirect to device Map to show you directions to get to that restaurant.

  • How to report a restaurant

  • Go to the restaurant page.
  • Tap at the upper right.
  • Choose the option that best describes the issue.
  • Confirm your decision.

  • How to contact a restaurant for reservation

    • The only way to contact the restaurant staff for reservation is to call them. We provide the contact number of the restaurant.
  • Go to the restaurant page. At the lower part, there’s a restaurant contact list.
  • Tap the phone number. The app will automatically dial it for you.

  • How can I add a review?

    • There are two ways to add a review.
    1. Through Restaurant Page
  • Go to the restaurant page where you want to add a review.
  • Tap .
  • Add rating, review title and your review (required). You can also attach photos (optional).
  • Enable Share to Facebook button if you want to share your review on your Facebook wall (optional).
  • Tap at the top right to post.
    • 2. Through Masarap Tab
  • Tap Masarap Tab.
  • Tap .
  • Choose the restaurant you want to add a review to.
  • Add rating, review title and your review (required). You can also attach photos (optional).
  • Enable Share to Facebook button if you want to share your review on your Facebook wall (optional).
  • Tap at the top right to post.

  • How can I add a photo?

    • You can add a photo through Restaurant Page.
  • Go to the restaurant page where you want to add a photo.

  • You can add a photo caption (optional).
  • Enable Share to Facebook button if you want to share your photo on your Facebook wall (optional).
  • Tap at the top right.
